CASA – Product Requirements Document (PRD)
1. Overview
Casa is a Nigerian real estate marketplace designed to help people find verified homes for rent or sale and help students easily find campus accommodations without dealing with fraudulent or expensive agents.
Casa aims to simplify the Nigerian property search process by providing a transparent platform where users can easily browse listings, compare prices, and contact property owners or agents directly.
The platform will initially launch as a mobile-first web application optimized for smartphone users.

2. Mission
Casa helps Nigerians find verified homes for rent and sale and also helps students find campus accommodations with ease without dealing with fraudulent or expensive agents.

3. Problem Statement
The Nigerian property market suffers from several major problems:
Too many middlemen and agents inflating prices
Lack of transparent pricing across listings
Limited ability to compare properties
Lack of verified listings
High rate of scams and fraudulent listings
Most platforms cater primarily to upper-class property seekers
Because of these issues, many Nigerians still rely on informal methods such as word of mouth or physical agents to find housing.
Casa aims to solve these problems by providing a transparent, accessible, and trustworthy property marketplace.
